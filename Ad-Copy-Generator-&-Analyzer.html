Ad Copy Analysis & Tips:

Analyze your copy for:

  • Strong Hook: Does it grab attention immediately?
  • Clear Problem: Is the pain point crystal clear and relatable?
  • Compelling Solution: Does your offer clearly solve the problem?
  • Single Call to Action: Is there one clear next step?
  • Readability: Is it easy to skim and understand?

4. Swipe File & Idea Bank

Borrow and adapt elements from these proven examples.

Hooks

  • "Are you tired of [pain]?"
  • "The #1 mistake [target audience] make..."
  • "What if you could [dream outcome] in just [timeframe]?"
  • "Discover the secret to [desired outcome] that [competitors] don't want you to know."
  • "Warning: If you're [target audience] and still [pain point], read this."
  • "Finally, a breakthrough that helps [target audience] [achieve desired outcome]!"
  • "Stop [negative action] and start [positive action] today!"
  • "The only guide you'll ever need to [solve specific problem]."
  • "Unlock the power of [concept] for [benefit]."

Problem Statements

  • "Stuck in the endless cycle of [ineffective method]?"
  • "Are you constantly battling [specific challenge] without seeing results?"
  • "The truth is, [common belief] is holding you back from [desired outcome]."
  • "Don't let [fear/obstacle] prevent you from achieving [goal]."
  • "Feeling overwhelmed by [complex task] and unsure where to start?"
  • "Is [current solution] costing you more time and money than it's worth?"
  • "Why are so many [target audience] failing at [task]?"
  • "The hidden costs of [avoiding solution] are crippling your [area]."

Solution/Benefit Statements

  • "Unlock your full potential and [achieve specific outcome]."
  • "Finally, a proven system to [solve problem] and get [desired result]."
  • "Experience the freedom of [positive feeling] with our [product/service]."
  • "Say goodbye to [pain point] and hello to [benefit]."
  • "Get [specific result] in [timeframe] with minimal effort."
  • "Transform your [area] with our innovative [feature/method]."
  • "Achieve [big goal] without [common struggle]."
  • "Your shortcut to [desired outcome] is here."

Social Proof / Credibility

  • "Trusted by over [number] successful [target audience]."
  • "Featured in [publication/media outlet]."
  • "See why [influencer name] calls this 'a game-changer'!"
  • "Our clients have seen [X%] increase in [metric] in just [timeframe]."
  • "Rated 5 stars by [number] happy customers."
  • "Join [number] others who have transformed their [area] with us."
  • "As seen on [TV channel/podcast]."
  • "Backed by [research/data/expert]."

Urgency / Scarcity

  • "Limited time offer: Ends [date/time]!"
  • "Only [number] spots left at this price!"
  • "Don't miss out on your chance to [benefit] – act now!"
  • "Price increases in [X] days – secure your access today."
  • "This exclusive offer won't last forever."
  • "Enrollment closes on [date]."
  • "The clock is ticking: [X] hours left!"
  • "Grab your bonus before it disappears!"

Call to Actions (CTAs)

  • "Click 'Learn More' to get started."
  • "Download the free guide now!"
  • "Limited spots! Secure yours before [date/time]."
  • "Get instant access: Click here!"
  • "Book your free consultation today."
  • "Start your free trial now!"
  • "Join our community and [achieve benefit]."
  • "Learn more and transform your [area]."
  • "Discover how we can help you [achieve goal]."
